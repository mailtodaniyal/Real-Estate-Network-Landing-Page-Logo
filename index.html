Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about DubaiBrokers.com

Who can join DubaiBrokers.com?

DubaiBrokers.com is exclusively for licensed real estate brokers operating in Dubai. All members must be verified with a valid RERA license before gaining full access to the platform.

How much does it cost to join?

Basic membership is completely free. We offer premium subscription plans with additional features, but you can access core networking and listing features at no cost.

How are the A2A rewards calculated?

Rewards are based on verified agent-to-agent contracts closed through our platform each month. Both parties must confirm the deal, and our team verifies the transaction before rewards are issued.

What can I use DB coins for?

DB coins can be redeemed for cash (1000 coins = 100 AED), used to boost your listings, purchase premium features, or enter exclusive competitions with valuable prizes.

How do I get verified?

After signing up, you'll need to upload a copy of your RERA license and a government-issued ID. Our verification team typically completes the process within 1-2 business days.
